About Me

I'm Emily, a graduate student at the Kelley School of Business pursuing a master's in information systems. I also have a master's in HCI/d and a bachelor's in Informatics. I care about building accessible, thoughtful experiences that make complex tools feel simple.

Interests include: human-computer interaction, product strategy, data-informed design, and music. Outside of work, I enjoy playing the drums, playing tennis, and watching movies.

Goals: In the next year, I'm aiming to deepen my skills in digital transformation, contribute to bettering myself and those around me, and learn skills that will benefit me as I enter the professional world.
